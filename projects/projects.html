Welcome to my projects' page! Here are a few of the projects that I've completed or at least remembered to take photos of. None of these are in any particular order and there are a lot of other projects that I've done and have forgotten to take photos of. If you have any questions about any of the projects, please feel free to contact me through the homepage. Happy browsing!
National Park Sign-
National Park them sign made for my brother and sister in law's newborn. Designed the sign on illustrator and traced and picture out on a cut piece of mdf. My wife painted the piece and I sealed it.
Guest Bathroom-
My mother-in-law wanted to remodel her guest bathroom and I figured it'd be a fun learning experience.
Car Roof Lining-
A friend had her car roof lining drooping and I offered to try to fix it. Its not a perfect fix but it isn't drooping anymore.
Wedding Arch-
Arch made for my wedding. Came up with several designs and had my wife pick one. Made a smaller proto type out of foam board to test dimensions and proportions. Made the rest out of wood and designed the arch in a way to be disassembled
Wedding Marquee-
This was a fun project! Designed the marquee based on me (Davis) and my wife's (Janika) name. This project was made out of MDF, led christmas lights, chalk poster board, and some glue paint.
Wedding Coasters-
Didn't know what to get for our guest for our wedding and decided to make some concrete coasters.
Ukelele Stand-
Had extra MDF and decided to make a ukelele stand for my father in law.
Small Desk-
Didn't have a desk and decided to make one myself. I think with all of the materials, it came out to under $30. This was made before the pandemic, where wood prices were not overpriced.
Sofa Table-
Another fun project. Helped my brother in law make this for his old sofa.
Indoor Ramp-
Made this for my grandmother. She had trouble using her walker to go to her patio. This was also designed to be able to be disassembled.
Laptop Sleeve-
Needed a laptop sleeve and decided to make my own.
Paper Wallet-
Had my wallet stolen, so I made a temporary one out of paper. Wallet is based on Bellroy's Slim Sleeve.
S2000 Glovebox Organizer-
Easy to make organizer. Made from foam board and toothpicks.
